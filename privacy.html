Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Pip's Playground is a learning game for young children. The short version: it doesn't collect anything, because it doesn't need to.

No accounts, no personal information

There is no sign-up, no login, and no profile. The app never asks for a name, email address, birthdate, photo, location, or any other personal information — from a child or from a parent.

What's stored, and where

Progress (crowns earned, stickers collected, daily streak, and the sound on/off setting) is saved using your device's local storage — the same technology any website uses to remember a setting. This data:

  • never leaves the device — it is not sent to us or to anyone else
  • is not linked to any identity
  • can be erased at any time from the in-app Parent Area (press and hold the gear icon on the map) with the "Reset All Progress" button, or by clearing the browser/app's site data

No ads, no analytics, no third parties

Pip's Playground has no advertising, no in-app purchases, no analytics SDKs, and no third-party trackers of any kind. All art, sound effects, and voice clips are bundled with the app, so it works fully offline and makes no network requests to anyone — including us — once it's loaded.

Children's privacy

This app is designed for young children and is built around that from the ground up: nothing is collected, so there is nothing to access, correct, or delete on a child's behalf. We comply with the spirit of COPPA and similar children's privacy laws by simply not gathering any data in the first place.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the update will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
